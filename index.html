Department of External Affairs

Information about our mission and operations

Our Mission

The Department of External Affairs is responsible for monitoring, analyzing, and establishing diplomatic relations with Groups of Interest (GOIs) outside the SCP Foundation.

Key Responsibilities

  • Tracking and documenting all known GOIs
  • Establishing communication protocols
  • Assessing threat levels and intentions
  • Coordinating with other Foundation departments
  • Managing diplomatic relations with neutral and friendly GOIs

Current Priorities

Monitor hostile GOI activities and maintain neutral/friendly relations where possible. Current focus is on containing potential XK-Class scenarios originating from hostile GOIs.
